Installing Mods

Overview · Installing · Eden Imports · Creating .ndx · nxmod.toml · Profiles · Cache · Cheats · GSO/GSC · Repositories · Safety · AI Guide

  1. Open Nx86.
  2. Drag a mod archive or folder onto the Nx86 window.
  3. Nx86 reads the Title ID from the manifest or folder structure. If it can't determine the game, it asks.
  4. Nx86 validates the mod. Malformed or suspicious archives are rejected with no partial write.
  5. Nx86 imports the mod into that game's cache folder and shows the rebuild cost.
  6. Launch the game. Any required rebuild runs first and is cached for future launches.
